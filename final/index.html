Rules

Artist Album Button
  • Hover On
    • Cursor Change: The cursor changes from a pointer to a hand, indicating interactivity.
    • Audio Activation: The album snippet begins to play immediately from the start.
    • Visual Glow: A soft purple glow radiates around the album image, highlighting it.
  • Hover Off
    • Cursor Change: The cursor changes back from a hand to a pointer.
    • Audio Fade & Stop: The snippet fades out quickly and stops completely.
    • Glow Reset: The purple glow softly fades away, returning the album image to normal.

Download Icon
  • Click On
  • Click Off

Rules

Download Icon
  • Click On
    • Color Change: The icon changes from gray to green (#1ed760).
    • Pulse Animation: The icon emits a soft pulsing glow for three seconds to simulate downloading.
    • Lock State: During the pulse animation, additional clicks are disabled.
  • Click Off
    • Color Reset: The icon returns to gray.
    • Pulse Stop: The pulse animation stops immediately.
    • Interaction Reset: The button can now be clicked again.

Feedback

Download Icon
  • Click On
    • Color Change: The icon changes from gray to green (#1ed760).
    • Pulse Animation: The icon emits a soft pulsing glow for three seconds to simulate downloading.
    • Lock State: During the pulse animation, additional clicks are disabled.
    • Backend: In practice, would download the album to the Spotify profile.
  • Click Off
    • Color Reset: The icon returns to gray.
    • Pulse Stop: The pulse animation stops immediately.
    • Interaction Reset: The button can now be clicked again.
    • Backend: In practice, would remove the download of the album from the Spotify profile.

Loops & Mode

Download Icon
  • Loops
    • The pulse animation temporarily loops for three seconds when activated.
  • Mode
    • Default Mode: Gray static icon (inactive).
    • Active Mode: Green glowing icon (download complete).
    • Locked Mode: While pulsing, the button cannot be clicked.

Triggers

Add to Library
  • Click (Add Album)
  • Click (Remove Album)

Rules

Add to Library
  • Click (On Add)
    • The album cover image briefly detaches and flies toward the “Add” icon (top-right).
    • The plus icon scales up slightly as if catching the album.
    • Once the album reaches the icon, it fades out and the plus turns into a green checkmark.
  • Click (On Remove)
    • The checkmark morphs back into a plus icon, returning to default state.
    • A reverse “fly out” animation sends the album cover back to its original position.

Feedback

    • The plus icon scales up slightly as if catching the album.

Triggers

Play Button
  • Click (Play)
  • Click (Pause)

Rules

Play Button
  • On Play
    • Album flips 180° along Y-axis to show back side.
    • Vinyl record spins smoothly in center.
    • Play button pulse syncs with record rotation.
    • Auditory: Album starts to play.
  • On Pause
    • Vinyl slows down and stops spinning.
    • Album flips back to front side.
    • Auditory: Album goes on pause.
